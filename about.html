About Our Department

Discover our rich history, mission, and commitment to excellence in computer science education and research.

Our Mission

The Computer Science Department is dedicated to providing world-class education in computing and information technology. We strive to:

  • Deliver cutting-edge curriculum that prepares students for successful careers in technology
  • Conduct innovative research that advances the field of computer science
  • Foster collaboration between academia and industry
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in STEM fields
  • Contribute to the technological advancement of society

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in computer science education and research, producing graduates who are innovative problem-solvers, ethical leaders, and lifelong learners capable of addressing the technological challenges of tomorrow.

Department History

1983 - Foundation

The Computer Science Department was established with faculty members and students, focusing on fundamental computing principles.

1995 - Expansion

Major expansion with new research labs and introduction of specialized tracks in AI and Software Engineering.

2005 - Innovation Hub

Launch of the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center, fostering startup culture among students and faculty.

2015 - Global Recognition

Achieved top 50 ranking nationally and established international partnerships with leading universities.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Successfully transitioned to hybrid learning models and launched online degree programs.

2025 - Future Ready

Introduction of Quantum Computing and Advanced AI specializations, preparing for the next technological revolution.
