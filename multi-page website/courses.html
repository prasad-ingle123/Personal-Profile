Course Catalog

Explore our comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare you for success in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.

Undergraduate Programs

🎓 Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 4 years (120 credit hours)

Core Areas: Programming, Algorithms, Data Structures, Software Engineering, Computer Systems

Specializations Available:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science

🎓 Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Duration: 4 years (120 credit hours)

Core Areas: Network Administration, Database Management, Web Development, IT Project Management

Career Focus: IT Management, Systems Administration, Web Development, Database Administration

Graduate Programs

🎓 Master of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 2 years (36 credit hours)

Options: Thesis or Non-thesis track

Research Areas: AI, Cybersecurity, Data Science, HCI, Quantum Computing

🎓 Ph.D. in Computer Science

Duration: 4-6 years

Requirements: Coursework, Comprehensive Exams, Dissertation Research

Funding: Research and Teaching Assistantships Available

Course Registration Information

📅 Academic Calendar

Fall Semester: August - December

Spring Semester: January - May

Summer Session: June - July

📋 Prerequisites

Students must complete all prerequisite courses with a grade of C or better before enrolling in advanced courses.

Academic Advisor: Required meeting before registration

Override Requests: Submit to department office
